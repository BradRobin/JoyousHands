Our Work

At Joy of Friendship for Children Programme, we are committed to improving the lives of underprivileged children through education, health initiatives, and community support. Hereâ€™s an overview of the projects we focus on:

Education

We believe education is the key to a brighter future. Our educational initiatives provide scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship to children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Health Initiatives

We offer health programs that include providing sanitary towels to young girls in schools and raising awareness about HIV/AIDS. We also focus on improving overall community health through outreach and health education.

Community Empowerment

Our work extends to training women and young vulnerable girls in entrepreneurship and empowering disabled individuals to earn a living. We provide start-up kits, and our follow-up programs ensure sustainable growth.

FGM Awareness

We actively advocate against Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) in communities that still practice it. Through education and awareness programs, we work with local leaders to change this harmful practice.

Support for Orphans

We provide uniforms, school fees, and emotional support to orphans to help them build a bright future.
