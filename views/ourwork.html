Our Mission

Since 2009, Joy of Friendship has been committed to addressing societal challenges and transforming lives. Our core mission revolves around providing assistance and opportunities to underprivileged groups.

What We Do:

  • Provide sanitary towels to young girls in schools and villages
  • Assist poor and needy children with school fees through partial or full scholarships
  • Train women and young vulnerable girls in entrepreneurship
  • Train disabled individuals on income-generating activities and offer follow-up support
  • Empower people with disabilities by providing start-up kits
  • Advocate and raise awareness on people living with disabilities
  • Combat female genital mutilation (FGM) through awareness campaigns
  • Spread awareness about HIV/AIDS
  • Provide uniforms to orphans
