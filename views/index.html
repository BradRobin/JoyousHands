Our Mission

At Joy of Friendship for Children Programme, we are committed to making a positive impact in society

Our Objectives

  • Providing sanitary towels to young girls in schools and villages.
  • Assisting needy children with school fees through partial or full scholarships.
  • Training women and young vulnerable girls in entrepreneurship.
  • Training people with disabilities on income-generating activities and making follow-ups to track progress.
  • Empowering people with disabilities with start-up kits to improve their quality of life.
  • Advocating and creating awareness for people living with disabilities.
  • Creating awareness to combat FGM in communities that still practice it.
  • Raising awareness about HIV/AIDS.
  • Providing uniforms to orphans.
