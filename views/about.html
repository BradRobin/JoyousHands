What We Do

The Joy of Friendship for Children Programme was started in 2009 with a mission to contribute to solving societal problems. Our key objectives include:

  • Providing sanitary towels to young girls in schools and the village
  • Assisting the poor and needy children by paying their school fees through partial or full scholarships
  • Training women and young vulnerable girls in entrepreneurship
  • Training people with disabilities in income-generating activities and conducting follow-ups to monitor progress
  • Empowering people with disabilities by providing start-up kits for a better life
  • Advocating for and raising awareness of people living with disabilities
  • Creating awareness against FGM in communities that still practice it
  • Raising awareness about HIV/AIDS
  • Providing uniforms to orphans
