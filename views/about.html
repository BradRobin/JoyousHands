Welcome to Joy of Friendship for Children Programme

We are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of underprivileged children. Founded in 2009, our organization has been working tirelessly to provide essential resources, opportunities, and support to children in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower children from disadvantaged backgrounds by providing them with essential resources, education, and support to achieve their full potential. We are dedicated to creating opportunities for a brighter future for every child we serve through education, health initiatives, and community programs.

Who We Are

Founded in 2009, Joy of Friendship for Children Programme began with a simple yet profound goal: to ensure that no child is left behind. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted organization with a deep commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of disadvantaged children.

Our Values

Compassion

We believe in treating every child with kindness and empathy.

Integrity

We operate transparently and uphold the highest standards of honesty.

Commitment

We are dedicated to long-term solutions and sustainable support for the children we serve.

Community

We work closely with families, local communities, and partners to create a supportive network.
